═══ DECISION TABLE — use after EVERY test ═══

  E1=HOLD  E2=HOLD  →  no separation → next test

ONLY the second row (E1=HOLD, E2=FAIL) is separation.
The third row (E1=FAIL, E2=HOLD) is NOT separation — do not confuse it.
